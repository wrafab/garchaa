    • Making a Personalized Skateboard Deck




    Lamination



    First, before gluing, I used the vinyl printer to print a sticker of super mario to put on my board



    7 layers of pre-cut wooden boards were glued together in a specific order




    Vacuum Forming



    Before the glue dried up, I used vacuum forming to form the skateboard.
    After placing the board on the mold, I taped them together to make sure
    the skateboard stay in place
    Then I placed it in a vacuum forming bag
    Before closing the bag, I placed a mesh on the board in order to
    make sure that the air in every corner comes out evenly.
    I closed up the bag, and pumped the air out
    This process took me a while because I was pumping out the air
    without realizing the bag wasn't sealed completely.





    Decorating




    After the skateboard was formed into the shape, the edges were all ugly, so I saned down the edges using different sanders.



    After I sand, the edges were now all smooth.




    For the surface, I used the surface sander and a block sander.


    After all the sanding process, I wiped the board using wet paper towel to get rid of all the dusts
    Then I placed the sticker on the bottom of the board and coated my board with the SPAR Urethane.


    And this is my final product of my board.
