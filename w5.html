3-D Printing and Scanning

Week 7 | 3-D Printing and Scanning

For this weeks project we had to mix 2 objects together on Meshmixer and print them out on the MakerBot 3-D printer. My idea was to design an Armadillo wearing a top hat. This process proved easy enough, however the outcome came out faulty since he ended up without a face. Another major problem also came up due to my miss understanding of non-subtractive made object. I instead created a design that can be made, but just not on our own Makerspaces 3-D printer. Design and limitations of the Makerbot Replicator 5th Gen : 1. Dimensional Constraints 2. Adative Manufactoring 3. Only on X,Y, Z Plane

Meshmixer and Fusion 360

To create the armadillo with a hat I used meshmixer. However, to create the ball with the whole I used Fusion 360.
