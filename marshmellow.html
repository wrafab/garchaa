    Reflection



    We had a problem of stabilizing the tower. Especially with the pennies inside, and the tower lost its balance and soon collapsed. I believe we focused too much on getting the tower attached to the cup instead of building up the base. Next time if we get a chance to build it again, I would add more sticks for support rather than adding them up to get higher so that will support pennies in a more stable way.
